About Us!

We are a group of students with a passion to share our knowledge! I chose to teach you one of my favorite recipes that my boyfriend continues to relentlessly beg me to bake!

After trying out dozens of cookie recipes on Pinterest, I can proudly say, this one is the tastiest!

Shake it while you Bake it!

I go to the University of Montana, and when I am not in class, I am baking cookies! I'm willing to try just about any recipe, but somehow I always find myself coming back to make these gingersnaps.

The original recipe calls for orange zest in the dough, but of all the times I made the cookies, I could not tell when the orange zest was present in the cookies, and acquiring the zest became more of a hastle than it waws worth!
